01

Services

Simple Design provides website design, website development, and website maintenance & hosting services for small businesses and individuals. All services are outlined and agreed upon before work begins.

We reserve the right to decline any project at our discretion.

02

Payment

All projects require a 50% deposit before work begins. The remaining 50% is due upon project completion, before the final website is delivered or made live.

Monthly services (Website Maintenance & Hosting) are billed at the start of each billing cycle. Failure to pay within 7 days may result in suspension of services.

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store card information.

Late payments: Invoices unpaid after 14 days are subject to a $25 late fee. Projects will not be delivered until balances are cleared.

03

Revisions

Each project includes two rounds of revisions at no additional charge. A revision round is defined as a consolidated list of changes submitted at one time.

Additional revision rounds beyond the included two are billed at $75/hour. Major scope changes (adding new pages, features, or sections not in the original agreement) are quoted separately.

Timeline & Delivery

Project timelines are estimated at the start of each engagement. Typical delivery is within 2 weeks of receiving the deposit and all required content (text, images, branding).

Any third-party assets (stock photos, fonts, plugins) remain subject to their respective licenses.

06

Website Transfer

Clients wishing to transfer their website to a new developer, host, or platform may do so at any time after full payment has been received.

Transfer Fee: $250
A one-time transfer fee of $250 applies to all website handoffs. This covers the preparation of all source files, documentation, account transfers, and a 30-minute handoff call to walk the new party through the project.

The transfer fee does not apply if the client is simply downloading their files for personal backup. It applies when transferring active management to a third party.

Monthly maintenance plans are automatically cancelled upon transfer. No refunds are issued for the current billing month.

07

Hosting & Maintenance Plans

Monthly maintenance plans include hosting, basic updates, and technical support. Plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled with 30 days written notice.

  • Hosting costs are included in the monthly fee
  • Minor content updates (text, images) are included
  • Major redesigns or new features are quoted separately
  • Cancellation requires 30 days written notice via email

Upon cancellation, clients receive all source files within 5 business days. A $250 transfer fee applies if files are being handed to a new developer.
