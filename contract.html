Payment Terms

All payments are processed via Stripe. A 50% deposit is required before work begins. The remaining 50% is due upon completion, before the site goes live. Invoices unpaid after 14 days are subject to a $25 late fee.

Agreement Terms

  • This contract includes 2 rounds of revisions at no additional charge. Additional rounds are billed at $75/hour.
  • The client retains full ownership of the website and all custom assets upon receipt of full payment.
  • Simple Design retains the right to display completed work in our portfolio unless requested otherwise in writing.
  • Projects cancelled after work begins forfeit the 50% deposit.
  • Typical delivery is within 2 weeks of deposit and receipt of all content (text, images, branding).

Website Transfer Fee: $250 — If the client wishes to transfer the website to a new developer, hosting provider, or manager at any time after project completion, a one-time $250 transfer fee applies. This covers source file preparation, documentation, account transfers, and a 30-minute handoff call.
